Welcome to the Arts Center

Where creativity meets innovation

The Arts Center at NYU Abu Dhabi is a dynamic space where performance, education, and community engagement come together to enhance the cultural life of the University and the city, fostering dialogue, creativity, and meaningful connections across disciplines and cultures.

Get Involved

Attend world-class performances, take part in workshops and talks, volunteer behind the scenes, or collaborate with artists from around the globe. From first-time audiences to experienced creatives, everyone is invited to be part of the conversation and contribute to a vibrant cultural community on campus and beyond.

Join us in shaping a space where ideas are shared, voices are heard, and creativity thrives. Your perspective matters—get involved and be part of the story.
